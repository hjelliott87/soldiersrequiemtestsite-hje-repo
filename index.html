Soldier's Requiem

by Henry James Elliott

Hello there traveler! Welcome to a lonely corner of the Internet. I imagine your curiosity brought here but I'll just cut to the chase.

This is Soldier's Requiem, an upcoming novel written by a random person named Henry James Elliott. Soldier's Requiem is a military drama set in the future where many individuals possess superpowers. The world is in a constant state of chaos, with war and terrorism tearing civilization apart. The year 2272, however, has been an unusually quiet year...

And in October of 2272, an unknown paramilitary terrorist force captures a top secert weapon from the United States with an unknown motive. An elite military force is summoned to track down and retrive the weapon before it is too late.

This is the story through the eyes of a young commander Reginleif Xavier, one of the leaders in charge of retriving the weapon from enemy hands. What twists and turns await her on the journey ahead...?

About the Author

Henry James Elliott is a Korean-born American who is currently a wandering vegabond. Orginally intending to become a video game developer, Elliott turned to writing as his passion shifted towards literature. Hobbies include video games, watching tv shows, writing random stuff and hockey. Elliott still dreams of producing video games and wants to bring a NHL team (as well as a MLB team) to San Antonio. Don't ask us why he wants that.
